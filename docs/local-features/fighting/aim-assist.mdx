# AimAssist
Assists with aiming at targets.

## Settings

### Range
Aim assist range.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Float`
* **Default Value**: `7.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `3.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `25.0`

---

### Players
Target players.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### Mobs
Target mobs.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### FOV
Field of View to limit targeting (degrees).

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Float`
* **Default Value**: `90.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `10.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `360.0`

---

### Speed
The Rotation Speed

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Float`
* **Default Value**: `1.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.5`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### Method
Rotation Method

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Enum`
* **Default Value**: `Linear`
* **Options**: `Linear`, `EaseIn`, `EaseOut`, `EaseInOut`, `Immediate`

---

### Priority
Sets the priority for target selection in Aim Assist.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Enum`
* **Default Value**: `Direction`
* **Options**: `Direction`, `Distance`, `Both`

---
