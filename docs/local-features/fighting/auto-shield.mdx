# AutoShield
Automatically shields against attacks.

## Settings

### DetectionRange
Sets the range for detecting incoming attacks.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `4.0`
* **Current Value**: `4.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### AutoEquip
Automatically equips a shield when an attack is detected.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`

---

### PredictionTicks
Number of ticks to predict enemy attacks.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `10`
* **Current Value**: `10`
* **Minimum Value**: `1`
* **Maximum Value**: `40`

---

### MeleeRangeMultiplier
Multiplier for melee attack detection range.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `1.5`
* **Current Value**: `1.5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.5`
* **Maximum Value**: `3.0`
