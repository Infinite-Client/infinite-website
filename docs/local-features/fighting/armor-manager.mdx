# ArmorManager
Automatically equips the best armor and manages elytra.

## Settings

### AutoEquip
Automatically equips the best available armor.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### ElytraSwitch
Automatically switches to elytra when airborne.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### DurabilityThreshold
Ignores armor with durability below this percentage (%).

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0`
* **Maximum Value**: `100`
