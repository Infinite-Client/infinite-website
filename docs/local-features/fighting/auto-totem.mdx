# AutoTotem

Automatically equips a totem when health is low.

## Settings

### Hp

HP at which totem is equipped.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Int`
- **Default Value**: `10`
- **Minimum Value**: `1`
- **Maximum Value**: `20`

---

### MinDelayMs

Minimum delay in milliseconds for Auto Totem.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Int`
- **Default Value**: `60`
- **Minimum Value**: `0`
- **Maximum Value**: `500`

---

### MaxDelayMs

Maximum delay in milliseconds for Auto Totem.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Int`
- **Default Value**: `180`
- **Minimum Value**: `0`
- **Maximum Value**: `500`
