# HyperMace
Enhances damage based on fall distance, for the mace weapon.

## Settings

### DamageBoost
Damage boost from Hyper Mace.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `4`
* **Minimum Value**: `0`
* **Maximum Value**: `10`

---

### FallMultiply
Damage multiply by Hyper Mace

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `1.5`
* **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `5.0`

---

### FallDistance
Minimum fall distance by Hyper Mace effect.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `500.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1000.0`
