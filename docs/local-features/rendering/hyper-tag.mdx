# HyperTag
Displays health bars and names above entities.

## Settings

### Mobs
Show Mob Info

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`

---

### Players
Show Player Info

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`

---

### Distance
Display Distance (0 to Unlimited)

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `64`
* **Current Value**: `64`
* **Minimum Value**: `0`
* **Maximum Value**: `256`

---

### Always
Always display nametags, regardless of distance.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`
* **Current Value**: `false`

---

### ShowItems
Display equipped items above entities.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`
* **Current Value**: `false`

---

### MinScaleDistance

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `32`
* **Current Value**: `32`
* **Minimum Value**: `1`
* **Maximum Value**: `256`

### FadeStartDistance

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `30`
* **Current Value**: `30`
* **Minimum Value**: `1`
* **Maximum Value**: `256`

* **Maximum Value**: `256`
