# FreeCamera

Allows free movement of the camera independently of the player.

## Settings

### Speed

Sets the camera movement speed.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Float`
- **Default Value**: `1.0`
- **Current Value**: `1.0`
- **Minimum Value**: `0.1`
- **Maximum Value**: `5.0`
