# CameraConfig

Configures camera distance, clipping, and anti-hurt tilt.

## Settings

### CameraDistance

Sets the distance between the camera and the player.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Float`
- **Default Value**: `8.0`
- **Minimum Value**: `-1.0`
- **Maximum Value**: `15.0`

---

### ClipBlock

Sets whether the camera penetrates terrain.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `true`

---

### AntiHurtTilt

Camera no longer shakes when taking damage.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `true`

---

### ExtraCamera

Camera no longer depends on player's orientation.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `false`
