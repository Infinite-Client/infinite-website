# AutoPilot
Auto Pilot System

## Settings

### TargetX
Target X coordinate for autopilot.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `0`
* **Minimum Value**: `-30000000`
* **Maximum Value**: `30000000`

---

### TargetZ
Target Z coordinate for autopilot.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `0`
* **Minimum Value**: `-30000000`
* **Maximum Value**: `30000000`

---

### ElytraThreshold
Durability percentage at which elytra will be swapped.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `5`
* **Minimum Value**: `1`
* **Maximum Value**: `50`

---

### SwapElytra
Automatically swaps elytra when durability is low or not equipped.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### StandardHeight
The standard height to maintain during flight.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `512`
* **Minimum Value**: `256`
* **Maximum Value**: `1024`

---

### RiseDirection
Sets the pitch angle for accelerating ascent.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `-45.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `-55.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `-35.0`

---

### GlidingDirection
Sets the pitch angle for altitude adjustment.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `20.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `5.7`
* **Maximum Value**: `40.0`

---

### FallDirection
Sets the pitch angle for decelerating descent.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `40.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `30.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `50.0`

---

### LandingDirectory
Sets the pitch angle for landing.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `-14.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `-45.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `0.0`

---

### EmergencyLandingThreshold
Remaining flight time (seconds) to trigger emergency landing.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `60`
* **Minimum Value**: `10`
* **Maximum Value**: `300`

---

### CollisionDetectionDistance
Distance (blocks) to check for terrain collision ahead.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `10`
* **Minimum Value**: `3`
* **Maximum Value**: `30`

---

### JetFlight
Enables jet flight mode for faster travel.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### JetSpeedLimit
Maximum speed in jet flight mode.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `30.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `10.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `50.0`

---

### JetAcceleration
Acceleration power for JetFlying. 0.0 for instant arrival.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `0.5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1.0`

---
