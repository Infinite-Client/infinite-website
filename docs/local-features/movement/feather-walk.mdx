# FeatherWalk

Prevents jumping and sprinting on certain blocks.

## Settings

### AllowedBlocks

List of block IDs to suppress jumping and sprinting on.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `BlockList`
- **Default Value**: `[minecraft:farmland]`
- **Item Count**: 1
- **List Type**: Block IDs

---

### DisableJump

Disables jumping on configured blocks.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `true`

---

### DisableSprint

Disables sprinting on configured blocks.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `true`
