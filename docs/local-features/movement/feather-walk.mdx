# FeatherWalk
Prevents jumping and sprinting on certain blocks.

## Settings

### AllowedBlocks
List of block IDs to suppress jumping and sprinting on.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `BlockList`
* **Default Value**: `[minecraft:farmland]`
* **Current Value**: `[minecraft:farmland]`
* **Item Count**: 1
* **List Type**: Block IDs

---

### DisableJump
Disables jumping on configured blocks.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`

---

### DisableSprint
Disables sprinting on configured blocks.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`
