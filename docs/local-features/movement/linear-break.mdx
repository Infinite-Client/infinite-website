# LinearBreak

Remembers all blocks targeted by the crosshair and breaks them quickly using packet transmission.

## Settings

### Range

Sets the range for LinearBreak.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Double`
- **Default Value**: `5.0`
- **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
- **Maximum Value**: `6.0`

---

### MaxBlocks

Sets the maximum number of blocks to break at once with LinearBreak.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Double`
- **Default Value**: `64.0`
- **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
- **Maximum Value**: `500.0`

---

### SwingHand

Sets whether to swing hand during LinearBreak.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `true`

---

### IgnoreHotbarChange

Sets whether to ignore hotbar changes during LinearBreak.

#### Setting Information

- **Type**: `Boolean`
- **Default Value**: `false`
