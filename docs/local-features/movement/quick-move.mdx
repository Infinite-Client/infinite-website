# QuickMove
Quickly accelerate and decelerate movement.

## Settings

### AccelerationConstant
Sets the base acceleration constant for QuickMove.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `0.02`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1.0`

---

### AccelerationMultiplier
Sets the acceleration multiplier for QuickMove.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `1.1`
* **Minimum Value**: `1.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `2.0`

---

### Friction
The rate at which movement speed decreases when no input is given.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `1.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1.0`

---

### Speed
The maximum speed achievable with QuickMove.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `0.75`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `2.0`

---

### ReductionThreshold
The reduction threshold of QuickMove

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `10.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `100.0`

---

### AntiFrictionBoost
Boosts movement speed by reducing friction.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `1.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `5.0`

---

### AntiFrictionPoint
Sets the point at which friction reduction is applied.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `0.75`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1.0`

---

### BoostWhenUseItem
Boosts movement speed when using an item.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `0.5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `1.0`

---

### AllowOnGround
Allows QuickMove to be active when on the ground.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### AllowOnSwimming
Allows QuickMove to be active when swimming.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowInWater
Allows QuickMove to be active when in water.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowInLava
Allows QuickMove to be active when in lava.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowInAir
Allows QuickMove to be active when in the air.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowOnGliding
Allows QuickMove to be active when gliding.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowWithVehicle
Allows QuickMove to be active when in a vehicle.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`
