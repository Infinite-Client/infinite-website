# SuperSprint
Forces sprinting even when hungry or moving backward.

## Settings

### OnlyWhenForward
Enables sprinting only when moving forward.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`
* **Current Value**: `true`

---

### EvenIfHungry
Forces sprinting even when hunger is low.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`
* **Current Value**: `false`
