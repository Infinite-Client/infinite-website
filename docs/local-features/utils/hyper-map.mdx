# HyperMap
Displays a mini-map on the screen.

## Settings

### Radius
Sets the radius of the mini-map display.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `32`
* **Minimum Value**: `5`
* **Maximum Value**: `256`

---

### Height
Sets the vertical height range displayed on the mini-map.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `8`
* **Minimum Value**: `1`
* **Maximum Value**: `32`

---

### Margin
Sets the margin of the mini-map UI from the screen edge.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `4`
* **Minimum Value**: `0`
* **Maximum Value**: `40`

---

### Size
Sets the size of the mini-map UI (% of screen short side).

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `40`
* **Minimum Value**: `5`
* **Maximum Value**: `100`

---

### Mode
Sets the display mode for the mini-map (Flat or Solid).

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Enum`
* **Default Value**: `Flat`
* **Options**: `Flat`, `Solid`

---

### RenderTerrain
Renders terrain on the mini-map.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### UseShading
Applies shading effects to the terrain rendering on the mini-map.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---
