# FoodManager
Automatically eats the best food in your inventory.

## Settings

### TargetHunger
The hunger level to maintain.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `10.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### MinHunger
The minimum hunger level before eating.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `6.5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### InjuredHunger
The hunger level to maintain when injured.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `10.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.0`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### InjuryThreshold
The health threshold to consider yourself injured.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Double`
* **Default Value**: `5.0`
* **Minimum Value**: `0.5`
* **Maximum Value**: `10.0`

---

### AllowRottenFlesh
Allows eating rotten flesh.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### AllowChorusFruit
Allows eating chorus fruit.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### PrioritizeHealth
Prioritizes health regeneration over hunger satisfaction.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `false`

---

### EatWhileMoving
Eat while moving

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### EatWhileAttacking
Sets whether to eat during attacks.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Boolean`
* **Default Value**: `true`

---

### SwapDelayTicks
Delay in ticks before swapping food.

#### Setting Information

* **Type**: `Int`
* **Default Value**: `5`
* **Minimum Value**: `0`
* **Maximum Value**: `20`

---
